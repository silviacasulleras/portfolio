Portfolio

These are selected side projects undertaken to explore topics outside my main research in theoretical quantum physics. They demonstrate my interest in computational modeling, simulation, optimization, and environmental data analysis. While some projects stem from coursework or small exercises, they reflect my curiosity and willingness to apply programming and analytical skills to new domains.

Optimal Control of a Levitated Quantum Particle

Designed static potentials to enhance quantum behavior in a levitated particle. Simulated the particle’s time evolution and analyzed its Wigner function using Python workflows for modeling, propagation, and constrained optimization.

Inverse Design of Pulses in Magnonic Waveguides

Developed pulses for magnonic waveguides using GPU-accelerated simulations. Combined numerical modeling with optimization techniques to shape pulses for controlling magnon dynamics.

Pulse Design for Qubit Control in Waveguides

Wildfire Impact and Vegetation Regeneration

A small Earth Observation project analyzing a wildfire in a chosen region of the Mediterranean. Tracked vegetation dynamics before, during, just after, and two years later using NDVI maps. This was a basic project, intended as a learning exercise, and not comparable in scope to my professional research work.
