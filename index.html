Postdoctoral Researcher in Theoretical Quantum Physics

I am a postdoctoral researcher in theoretical quantum physics with extensive experience in numerical modeling, optimization, and high-performance simulations. My work spans from designing quantum control protocols to developing tailored pulse sequences. This portfolio showcases a selection of projects that demonstrate my skills in computational modeling, simulation, data analysis, and my growing interest in Earth Observation.

Optimal Control of a Levitated Quantum Particle

Designed static potentials to enhance quantum behavior in a levitated particle. Simulated the particle’s time evolution and analyzed its Wigner function using Python workflows for modeling, propagation, and constrained optimization.
